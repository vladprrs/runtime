| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-01-04 | Denial of Service | clippy pedantic | mitigate | `priority = -1` on pedantic group with selective allows prevents CI false-positive blocks. Allows are explicit and documented. |
| T-01-05 | Information Disclosure | Cargo.toml | accept | Dependency list is not sensitive. Repository is private. No secrets in manifests. |
